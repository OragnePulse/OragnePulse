Shipping and Delivery Information

Last updated: December 2, 2025

1. Order Processing and Fulfillment

As a dropshipping company, **Oragné Pulse** relies on a global network of suppliers for fulfillment. This process involves two key stages: processing and shipping.

  • Processing Time: Once your order is placed, it takes **1–5 business days** for our suppliers to verify the order, perform quality checks, and package the items for shipment. This time is included in the total estimated delivery window.
  • Fulfillment Location: Items may be shipped from various locations, including within the EU and international hubs, depending on the product and supplier inventory.

2. Estimated Delivery Times (After Processing)

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to factors like customs, holidays, and carrier delays. Delivery days are calculated from the moment the item is shipped (after processing).

Region Standard Delivery Estimate
European Union (EU) 7 – 15 Business Days
United Kingdom 8 – 16 Business Days
Rest of World 10 – 25 Business Days

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on the weight, dimensions, and destination of your order. We occasionally offer promotional shipping rates, which will be clearly indicated on the website.

  • Calculation: Costs are calculated dynamically based on the supplier's shipping fee structure.
  • Multiple Shipments: If your order contains items from different suppliers, they may arrive in separate packages on different days. You will only be charged the total shipping fee calculated at checkout, regardless of the number of shipments.

4. Customs, Duties, and Import Taxes

We comply with EU VAT regulations, and all prices displayed for EU deliveries include VAT where applicable.

  • EU Deliveries: You will not be charged any additional customs or import duties upon delivery within the European Union.
  • Non-EU Deliveries: For orders shipped to destinations outside the EU, the customer is responsible for any import duties, customs fees, tariffs, or taxes levied by the destination country. These charges are outside our control and must be paid by the recipient.

5. Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a Shipping Confirmation email containing your tracking number(s). You can use this number on the carrier's website to monitor the progress of your delivery.

  • Tracking Updates: Please allow 24–48 hours for the tracking information to update after receiving the confirmation email.
  • Need Help? If your tracking information has not updated after 5 business days or if you believe your package is lost, please contact our support team immediately.
